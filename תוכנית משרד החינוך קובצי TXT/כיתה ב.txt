Grade 2 – Foundation Stage
Ministry of Education · English Curriculum 2020 (Elementary School)

Focus
- Move from exposure to controlled production: phonics, decoding and short utterances.
- Continue enjoyable listening while adding shared reading of simple words/sentences.
- Begin copying and spelling highly familiar items.

Skills
- Listen to short stories/chants and identify key words or pictures.
- Read CVC words and very short sentences supported by visuals.
- Speak using simple patterned sentences (“This is my …”, “I like …”).
- Copy/trace labels and write isolated words (names, classroom items, colors).

Grammar & Structures
- Simple present of “be” (I am / You are / He is / She is / It is).
- Personal pronouns, plural nouns with -s, demonstratives (this/that).
- Question patterns: “What is it?”, “Who is this?”

Vocabulary Themes
- Food and drinks, clothes, classroom routines.
- Animals (farm/pets), house and rooms, community places.
- Action verbs (run, jump, read, write, eat, play).
- Numbers 1–20, days of the week, simple weather words.

Benchmark – End of Grade 2
- Decode and read familiar words and short patterned sentences aloud.
- Respond orally to yes/no and wh- questions using memorized chunks.
- Copy or write words/labels related to the taught themes with correct spelling.
- Understand short listening texts that recycle the taught vocabulary and grammar.
